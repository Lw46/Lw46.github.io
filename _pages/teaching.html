Data description

An atmospheric correction process was performed for Band01 to 06 of the CEReS Grid AHI using the look-up table (LUT) method based on the Second Simulation of a Satellite Signal in the Solar Spectrum vector (6SV) radiative transfer model (RTM) and the Copernicus Atmosphere Monitoring Service Reanalysis (CAMSRA) - European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF) Atmospheric Composition Reanalysis 4 (EAC4). In the processing, a mean resampling method was used for the data of Band03 to generate LSRs of 0.01°. LSRs were obtained for each 10 minutes from UTC00 to 08 for 2018 and 2019 from ftp://modis.cr.chiba-u.ac.jp/ichii/SEND_NEW/H8AHI_SR/. To match the spatial resolution of the short wavelength infrared bands (Band 05 and 06), a mean down-sampling method was used for the data of Band 01 to 04 (2x2 pixels of 0.01° corresponding to a 0.02° pixel) to resample the resolution to 0.02°.

Cloud mask data were generated using the Yamamoto et al. (2018, 2022) algorithm, which sets thresholds for reflectance and brightness temperature in visible and infrared band data to identify clouds. The algorithm provides a cloud mask of the FD region with a spatial resolution of 0.02°.
